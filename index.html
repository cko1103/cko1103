Cycle (2023) - Student Thesis Film

Responsible for all aspects, using Houdini, Redshift, and Nuke.

Pitch

Somewhere in the vast universe, a supernova explosion occurs. An asteroid from the explosion lands on a nearby planet, triggering new life. My thesis film "Cycle" was inspired by the idea that we are all children of the stars, and life cycles through the universe.

When supernovae explode, these blasts produce much of the material in the universe - including some elements like iron, which make up our planet and even ourselves.

My thesis film "Cycle" was inspired by this idea, that we are all children of the stars, and life cycles through the universe.
